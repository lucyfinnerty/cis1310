My Collection

This here is a small portion of my ongoing CD collection in real life. I have about 60-70 in total, but these are my favorites. When an album is selected, you'll be transported to my number one song off that album. Each of these albums are 5/5s in my book, so I hope you can give them a try.



Recommendations

Album Rating Favorite Song Description

Selected Ambient Works 85-92 - Aphex Twins 		★★★☆☆ Xtal
Time: 4:53		 This album is a collection of ecletic sounds. Each song different from the one before and after it. I like to listen to this album while studying or doing tasks where I need to be completely focused.

Self-titled - Third Eye Blind 		★★★★☆ I Want You
Time: 4:29		 This album contains a lot of radio hits that encompass those 90s alternative/pop rock and post-grunge sounds. Every song is a hit and is very nostaglic -- reminds me of when I was a child in the backseat of my parent's cars, radio on.

Graffiti - Roy Blair 		★★★☆☆ Fantazia
Time: 5:53		 This album was a long time coming. Graffiti EP blew me away, but now having the complete project, I've come to love the kind of muscian Roy Blair is. His sounds are so unique and the timing/way he manipulates his voice is so genius.

Four Calendar Cafe - Cocteau twins 		★★★★☆ Know Who You Are At Every Age
Time: 3:40		 This album transports me into another dimension. Elizabeth Fraser, lead singer, has an ethereal voice which pairs well with the dream pop instrumentals. Each song is soft, subtle, and I feel like I'm laying in grass on a warm summer day, lemonade in hand.
